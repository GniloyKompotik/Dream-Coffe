Our Story

Coffee Time Cafe was opened in 2010 with the aim to offer the city's residents a cozy place to relax and enjoy delicious coffee and freshly baked goods. We take pride in providing our guests with excellent service and a warm atmosphere.

At our cafe, you can not only enjoy a cup of coffee but also hang out with friends, work, or have a romantic dinner.
